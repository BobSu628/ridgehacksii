Ridge Hacks

9am to 9pm Dec 3rd @ Iroquois Ridge High School




Details


Thank you for registering for Ridge Hacks, here is everything you need to know for the big day! You should arrive at at 9:00am at Iroquois Ridge High School on Saturday December 3rd to check in at the front desk, pick up some free swag, and make your way to the theatre to set up. The opening ceremonies will start at 10:00am and the event will end at 9:00pm.

Please bring:
- A laptop and charger
- A lunch (dinner and snacks will be provided)

You have the option to be in teams of up to 4, work on your own, or form teams with other participants during the event; you will have time to do so after the opening ceremonies. Throughout the day, you will be working on a “hack” that you will demo at the end of the day. Your “hack” must be created during the 12 hours, but we recommend you arrive with an idea to save time and get started right away.
