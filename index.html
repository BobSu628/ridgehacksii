About


Ridge Hacks is a Local Hack Day organized by MLH and Iroquois Ridge High School, other schools all over the world will be simultaneously hosting their own Local Hack Day, resulting in the largest day of student hacking ever. A Local Hack Day is a 12 hour event (similar to a hackathon) focused on learning computer science skills while creating cool projects. Learn more about Local Hack Day.

Ridge Hacks brings professionals and mentors from the community to teach workshops on the latest technology, the workshop are beginner friendly and open to all students. Ridge Hacks is for anyone who wants to learn more about computer science even if you have never been to a hackathon before. Best of all, Ridge Hacks is completely free!


Come join your local computer science community on December 3rd.

Questions


How much does it cost to attend?

Nothing! Ridge Hacks is completely free.


What should I bring?

A laptop, cables, charger are all that is needed. Stay tuned for information about food and drinks.


Who can attend?

Any university, high school, or middle school student may attend Ridge Hacks. Members of a school's staff may attend but must also register.


Do I need a team?

A team is not required although participants can be in teams up to 4, you do not need to register as a team. Particpants can come alone and form teams on the day.
